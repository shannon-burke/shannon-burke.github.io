Graphic Design


I've had a passion for design and print for most of my life. I'm skilled in the Adobe Creative Suite--namely Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign--but I'm always interested in new software and methods. I graduated from MWSU in 2016 with 2 BFAs in graphic design and illustration and currently work for a retail marketing company based out of the Kansas City area doing print, social media, and web based design.

Publications

Since early 2020 I've been working with a large client base of retail store on their seasonal mailed publications. Working directly with store owners has been a great (and sometimes trying) experience!

Social Media Images

In my current position a large portion of the client base aren't exactly tech-savvy, so any social media assets I create for client feeds have to speak for themselves.

Krei Creative Supplies

Krei is a creative supplies packaging project completed in 2015. Krei's target audience are young creatives drawn to simplicity and boldness. The brightly colored textures were all hand painted and combined with geometric shapes for a stark contrast.

Mailed Promotions

I've had the opportunity to work on a variety of promotional event advertisements, mostly in the form of flat cards. These are some of my faves!


Flyers & Posters

Assorted posters, flyers, infographics, and window decals.

Logos

Logos, emblems, and small graphic assets I've created for various clients and projects.

Small Bites Panini Recipes

Small Bites was a collaborative project I participated in as a student in 2015, where each team member contibuted several full page spreads as well as individual elements throughout the entier book.

Umami

Umami is a mock restaurant branding project I worked on in 2013. For the visual elements of this project I wanted to combine a blend of contemporary Japanese street culture along side some of the more traditional elements typically associated with sushi.

American Indian Museum Cards
