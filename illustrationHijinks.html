Hijinks & Handlebars Podcast Poster


Hijinks & Handlebars is an actual play Kids on Bikes podcast hosted by Queuetimes. The poster illustration below depicts the show's biggest battle, which takes place in a magical, dream-like realm called the undershade. Though the show certainly has its serious moments it is, at its heart, about the magic and whimsy of childhood and the early teens. I chose to keep my style and color choices aligned with that youthful, fun spirit in this piece.
