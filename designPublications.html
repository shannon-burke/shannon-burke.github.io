Publications


Since early 2020 I have worked with various small retailers on their main source of advertising to their customer base--mailed publications. I work on both trifold mailers and standard stapled catalogs. Working directly with store owners has been a great way to make sure the client gets exactly what they want and that I can make sure the ads catch as many customer eyes as possible!
